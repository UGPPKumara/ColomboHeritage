Welcome to Colombo, Sri Lanka!

Colombo is the largest city and commercial capital of Sri Lanka. It is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city with a rich history and culture. Colombo is home to many tourist attractions, including the Galle Face Green, the Gangaramaya Temple, the Pettah Market, and the Colombo National Museum.
